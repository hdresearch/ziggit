PHASE 1: Ziggit API vs CLI Benchmark Results
==============================================

Debug Mode (-Doptimize=Debug):
  Operation          | Zig Med | CLI Med | Speedup
  -------------------|---------|---------|--------
  rev-parse HEAD     | 76.9μs  | 1.02ms  | 13.3x  
  status --porcelain | 1.04ms  | 1.36ms  | 1.3x   
  describe --tags    | 140.5μs | 1.16ms  | 8.3x   
  is_clean           | 1.04ms  | 1.36ms  | 1.3x   

ReleaseFast Mode (-Doptimize=ReleaseFast):
  Operation          | Zig Med | CLI Med | Speedup
  -------------------|---------|---------|--------
  rev-parse HEAD     | 28.1μs  | 908.0μs | 32.3x  ⚡
  status --porcelain | 104.5μs | 1.26ms  | 12.1x  ⚡
  describe --tags    | 46.5μs  | 1.06ms  | 22.8x  ⚡
  is_clean           | 104.8μs | 1.26ms  | 12.0x  ⚡

Analysis:
- ReleaseFast shows 2-10x improvement over Debug
- Best performer: rev-parse HEAD (32.3x speedup)  
- Needs optimization: status operations (only 12x speedup)
- Target: 100-1000x speedup by eliminating all overhead

PHASE 2 Optimization Targets:
1. rev-parse HEAD: 28μs → should be ~1-5μs (2 file reads)
2. status --porcelain: 104μs → should be ~10-20μs (fast index/stat check)
3. describe --tags: 46μs → decent but could be faster with caching
